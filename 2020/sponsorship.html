2020

June 5–7, Ostrava

Sponsor­ship

The PyCon CZ 2020 organizers are pleased to invite your sponsorship of the fifth annual 3-day PyCon conference in Ostrava, Czech Republic, on 5‑7 June 2020. Sponsorship presents opportunities to connect you with up to 500 expected attendees.

Sponsorship benefits

Sponsorship packages start from 20000 CZK and can include:

  • Partner announced on the PyCon CZ official social media channels
  • Logo on the conference website
  • Logo on the intermission slide deck
  • Roll-up (standing banner) at the venue
  • Booth at the venue
  • Promotional items or leaflets in the swag bag of every atendee
  • Promotional posts
  • Retweets/reshares on the PyCon CZ official social media channels
  • Free tickets to the conference
  • Exclusive options: coffee, party drinks or lanyard sponsor

Download detailed sponsorship information in our prospectus (PDF)

Custom sponsorship

In the case that our sponsorship packages don’t fit youur needs, you can become a diversity sponsor (provide free tickets for a target demographic), host or contribute prizes for a quiz/competition, or become a sponsor of a connected event. And of course, if you have your own idea about a custom sponsorship, we’d love to hear it!

Event summary

  • Friday June 5th to Sunday June 7th 2020
  • 500 attendees (professionals, students and hobbyist Pythonistas)
  • 2 full conference days with 2 tracks primarily in English
  • Both tracks streamed online and recorded
  • 1 full day of workshops and sprints
  • 30+ diverse, international speakers
  • Extra talks & workshops for beginners in Czech
  • Broad range of both technical and community topics
  • Sponsor booths
  • Social events and parties
  • Organized by a community of volunteers
  • Friendly environment
  • Food and drinks on all 3 conference days
  • Gorgeous industrial venue in Ostrava
